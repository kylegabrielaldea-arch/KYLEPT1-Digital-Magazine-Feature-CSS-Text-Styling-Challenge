Artificial Intelligence in Education

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is becoming one of the most influential technologies in modern education. Many schools and learning platforms now use AI to help personalize lessons, assist teachers in grading, and provide students with instant feedback. These tools allow learners to study at their own pace while making education more accessible and efficient.

However, the growing use of AI also raises important questions about responsibility, academic integrity, and the balance between technology and human interaction in learning environments. Understanding both the benefits and challenges of AI is important as education continues to evolve in the digital age.

Author's Insight

In my opinion, artificial intelligence can greatly improve education when used responsibly. AI can support teachers and provide students with better learning opportunities, but it should not replace human guidance and critical thinking. Technology should serve as a tool that enhances education rather than becoming the sole source of knowledge.
