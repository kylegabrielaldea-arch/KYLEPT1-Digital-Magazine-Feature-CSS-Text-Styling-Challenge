Artificial Intelligence has become an important part of modern education. Many schools now use AI-powered tools to personalize learning, assist teachers, and provide students with better access to information.

However, the use of AI also raises concerns about academic honesty, technology dependence, and privacy. Students must learn how to use these tools responsibly while still developing their own critical thinking skills.
